Question 1: Is the login feature mandatory?
Ans. If you wish to reserve or book tickets via Airgo system, then yes. But if you wish to simply scroll through our offerings then feel free to do so.

Question 2. Do I need different passwords for Airgo and payment portals?
Ans. Yes, the password you choose for Airgo is essentially the one you'll need to access our site. The payment is authenticated and conducted by individual portals.

Question 3. Will I receive a refund on cancellation?
Ans. Although we at Airgo believe that the users must be our priority and their comfort is our prime concern, we do not deal with payments (as of now) so the refund policy of the selected Airline will have to be refered.

Question 4. In the homepage, we have a choice to book tickets directly on the airlines' website. How does this help Airgo?
Ans. Certain airline companies have supported Airgo and are valuable and constant partners with us.
